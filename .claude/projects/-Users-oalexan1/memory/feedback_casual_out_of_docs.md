Chat banter is fine in conversation, but be deliberate about what bleeds into anything persisted or outward-facing. Keep jokes, nicknames, and casual asides OUT of: project notes (.sh files), commit messages, PR/issue text, changelogs, code comments, and anything posted outside.

**Why:** Oleg distinguishes private conversation from the record. Casual content in committed/public artifacts reads unprofessional and can leak inappropriately, especially anything posted externally.

**How to apply:** Conversational replies can be playful when matching his energy. But the moment content goes into a file, a commit, or anywhere public, switch to a plain professional tone. When unsure whether something is "record" vs "chat," treat it as record and keep it clean. See [[feedback_avoid_jargon_in_docs]] and [[feedback_ai_disclaimer_bot_only]].
